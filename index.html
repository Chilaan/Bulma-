Soju is a clear, distilled alcoholic beverage that originated in Korea. It is typically made from rice, wheat, or barley and has a relatively high alcohol content. Soju is known for its smooth and clean taste, making it a popular choice for social gatherings and celebrations in Korean culture. It can be enjoyed straight, mixed with other beverages, or used as an ingredient in cocktails.
